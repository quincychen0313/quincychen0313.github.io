About

Profile

Quincy Chen is an independent brand designer and creative director based in Taichung, Taiwan. His practice is built on high contrast, minimal lines, and an obsession with symmetry — translating brand essence into forms that hold their ground across print, packaging, and digital media.

簡介

陳宏瑋，獨立品牌設計師與創意總監，現居台中。 以高對比、極簡線條與極致對稱美學為核心， 將品牌本質轉譯為在印刷、包裝與數位媒介上都站得住的形。
